Lamb Karahi:

Ingredients
Meat
• 1 3/4 lbs Lamb on the bone
Produce
• 2 Chillies, dry red
• 2 Chillies, green
• 1 sprig Cilantro or parsley
• 1 tsp Ginger juliennes
• 1 Onion, medium
Condiments
• 4 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
Baking & Spices
• 1 tbsp Coriander seeds
• 1/4 tsp Peppercorns
• 1 Sea salt
Oils & Vinegars
• 1/3 cup Cooking oil
Nuts & Seeds
• 1 tsp Cumin seeds
Other
• 4 5 to matoes (chopped)
